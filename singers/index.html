Diljit Dosanjh

Born 1984

Daljit Singh Dosanjh, popularly known as Diljit Dosanjh, is a singer, actor, and television presenter who works in the Hindi and Punjabi film industry. He is a leading artiste in the music industry in Punjab.Dosanjh spent the initial days of his life in Dosanjh Kalan before moving to Ludhiana, Punjab.
